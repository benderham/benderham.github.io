ClimateSERV

ClimateSERV allows scientists and government decision-makers to visualise rainfall data, the condition of global plant life, along with 180-day forecasts of rainfall and temperature.

Temperature This Month

Global Imagery Browse Services

This system shows historical and real-time daily global imagery taken from space. The service provides colour pictures and climate data that includes Sea Surface Temperature, Sea Ice Concentration and many other useful data points.

Find out more about GIBS

Current Natural Disasters

NASA’s EONET
(Earth Observatory Natural Event Tracker)

EONET is a repository of metadata about natural events. The service lists natural event data in real time. By analysing this data NASA can action early warning systems and track natural disasters over time. Less natural disasters is better for life on Earth.

Find out more about EONET
